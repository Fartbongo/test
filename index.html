About Me

A passionate bassist, Nathan Clark brings a unique blend of rhythm and melody to every performance. With a rich career that spans twenty years, Nathan has played with numerous bands and artists, crafting a distinctive sound that resonates with audiences. Known for his dynamic performances in numerous venues across New York City, Nathan continues to push the boundaries of musical expression.

    Blog

    2/2/2025
    Check out my new album "Oops! All Jameos! Vol. 1" on the music page!
    I have been having fun doing simple recordings of me playing drums, bass, and guitar one at a time, one man band as they say. This new album is a recording of me having fun doing that and seeing creative sparks fly. I know there are plenty of "mistakes" in the recording that may piss some people off or give haters fuel but oh well.

    I have always wanted to capture that live feel of a band playing in a room together, and I think this is a good way to do it. The later tracks are ones that I made at first just fiddling around but I realized that the trio type sound fits what I am going for. It is also much easier to mix and record with just drums bass and guitar, I think adding things like keys and synths muddle what I am trying to do. Too many choices and I get into an identity crisis.

    It has always been hard to get likeminded musicians to cooperate with each other, this one man band thing has been a development of my frustration with that. The drums are me playing them on a keyboard, I have a midi keyboard that I use to write music with and I have been getting better at it. I still don't know what the hell I am doing as a drummer.
