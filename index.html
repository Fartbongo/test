About Me

A passionate bassist, Nathan Clark brings a unique blend of rhythm and melody to every performance. With a rich career that spans twenty years, Nathan has played with numerous bands and artists, crafting a distinctive sound that resonates with audiences. Known for his dynamic performances in numerous venues across New York City, Nathan continues to push the boundaries of musical expression.
